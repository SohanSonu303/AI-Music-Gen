# AI Music Gen — Functional Requirements

A friendly tour of every feature in AI Music Gen, written for the people who use it. This document is the single source of truth that powers our help chatbot, and it's organised so you can jump straight to whatever you're trying to do.

If you're new here, start with **Generation** to make your first track, then explore **Editing** to shape it, **Production** to make it release-ready, and **Account & Workspace** for everything around your library and billing.

---

## Generation

Tools that turn an idea, a prompt, or a piece of source material into brand-new music, lyrics, or sound.

### Music Generation

**What it does.** Music Generation creates original songs from a short text prompt. You describe the mood, genre, tempo, or feeling you want — for example *"a chill lo-fi beat with warm piano and rain in the background"* — and the engine returns two complete tracks for you to choose between, each with title, audio, duration, and (where applicable) generated lyrics.

**How it benefits you.** You get publishable, full-length music in minutes instead of days. Two takes per request means you almost always have a usable option without re-prompting, and each track lives in your library ready to download, edit, or remix. Perfect for content creators, indie filmmakers, app developers, podcasters, and anyone who needs original, royalty-free music on demand.

### Lyrics Generation

**What it does.** Lyrics Generation writes original song lyrics from a prompt. You can guide the result with optional **style**, **mood**, **theme**, and **tone** fields — for example *"upbeat indie-pop, hopeful, summer road-trip, conversational"* — and you receive a complete lyric ready to use, edit, or send to Music Generation as the words for a song.

**How it benefits you.** Beating writer's block becomes a matter of seconds. You can spin up dozens of lyric drafts to compare, lock in the one you love, and pair it with one of our music generation features. Songwriters, hobbyists, and creators get a tireless co-writer available 24/7.

### Quick Idea

**What it does.** Quick Idea takes a short brief (up to 280 characters) and returns a tight, ready-to-prompt music concept covering mood, genre, tempo, and a hook. Think of it as a creative spark: you say *"something for a cooking video"* and you get back a concise, focused concept like *"warm acoustic, mid-tempo 95 BPM, brushed drums, gentle ukulele hook, cosy and inviting."*

**How it benefits you.** When you're stuck staring at an empty prompt box, Quick Idea gives you a strong starting point in one click. It's the fastest way from "I have no idea" to "I have something I can actually generate."

### Prompt Enhancer

**What it does.** Prompt Enhancer takes a basic music prompt and rewrites it into a richer, production-ready version. You type *"sad piano song"*, and the enhancer returns something like *"melancholic solo grand piano in A minor, slow tempo around 65 BPM, sparse left-hand accompaniment, intimate close-mic feel, gentle reverb tail."* You can also supply your own master prompt template if you have a specific house style.

**How it benefits you.** Better prompts produce better music. The enhancer translates your rough creative direction into the concrete musical detail the generation engine responds best to — so the track you get back is closer to what you imagined, with fewer retries.

### Sound Effect Generation

**What it does.** Sound Effect Generation creates short sound effects (SFX) from a text prompt — *"old wooden door creaking open"*, *"sci-fi laser blast"*, *"crowd cheering in a stadium"* — at any length you choose. The result is delivered as a clean audio file in your library.

**How it benefits you.** No more digging through generic stock-effect libraries hoping something fits. You describe the exact sound you need and get a unique, royalty-free clip tailored to your scene. Filmmakers, game developers, podcasters, and animators save hours of search time on every project.

### Album Generation

**What it does.** Album Generation turns a written script — like a story, a film treatment, a game outline, or a chapter list — into a complete multi-track album. You decide how many songs, background scores, and instrumentals you want (between 1 and 20 tracks total), and the planner reads the script, segments it into scenes, picks an emotional arc and a coherent style palette, then writes a tailored prompt and (for vocal tracks) full lyrics for every track. You review and edit the plan, approve it, and the system generates all the tracks.

**How it benefits you.** Scoring a whole project — an audiobook, a short film, a podcast season, an indie game — is reduced from weeks of work to a guided, hour-long workflow. You stay in the driver's seat: you can re-plan any track, regenerate any track that doesn't land, and update titles or lyrics before approving. The result is a cohesive album where every track is matched to its scene.

### Image to Song

**What it does.** Image to Song generates a piece of music inspired by a picture you upload. The system reads the visual mood, colours, and content of the image and turns that into a music prompt, then produces a song that matches the vibe of what you uploaded.

**How it benefits you.** Sometimes you can't describe what you want in words, but you know it when you see it. Drop in a sunset photo, a moody concept-art piece, or a screenshot from your game, and you get music that fits the feeling. Great for visual creators who think in images first.

### Inpaint

**What it does.** Inpaint lets you regenerate a specific section *inside* an existing track without touching the rest. Pick the moment that isn't working — a dull bridge, a clumsy transition, an out-of-place verse — and the system fills it back in with a new musical passage that flows seamlessly with the audio on either side.

**How it benefits you.** You no longer have to throw away a great track because of one weak section. Inpaint is the audio equivalent of "fix this part" — quick, surgical, and preserves everything you already love about the song.

### Extend

**What it does.** Extend takes any track in your library and adds new musical material at the end, continuing the style, key, and mood of the original. You choose where the extension begins, and the system generates a smooth continuation — perfect for turning a short clip into a full track, or stretching an outro for a longer scene.

**How it benefits you.** A 60-second demo can become a 3-minute song without re-recording or re-prompting. If you've ever needed "just a bit more" of a piece — to fit a video edit, to fill a level, to round out an album — Extend gets you there without breaking the vibe.

### Remix

**What it does.** Remix takes one of your existing tracks and reimagines it. You can change the prompt, swap or rewrite the lyrics, change the vocal gender, or just say *"give me a different take"* — and the system produces a fresh interpretation of the original. The source audio guides the new version, so the remix shares DNA with the track you started from.

**How it benefits you.** One source can fuel dozens of variations: a cinematic version for a trailer, an acoustic version for a quiet scene, a club edit for a high-energy moment, a vocal swap for a different language. Remix multiplies the value of every song you create.

## Editing

Tools to shape, polish, and reshape audio you've generated or uploaded. All editing happens locally and in real time — preview as you go, save when you're happy.

### Cut / Fade / Loop / Split / Mix / Overlay / EQ

**What it does.** This is the core audio editing toolkit. **Cut** trims a section out of a track. **Fade** adds smooth fade-in or fade-out curves. **Loop** repeats a passage to fill a target length. **Split** divides a track into two parts at a chosen moment. **Mix** combines two clips into one. **Overlay** places one clip on top of another at a chosen position. **EQ** boosts or cuts specific frequencies so you can brighten a dull mix or tame a harsh one. Every operation runs on the audio you uploaded or generated, with a live waveform display so you can see exactly where you're editing.

**How it benefits you.** You don't need a separate digital audio workstation to make basic edits. Trim the silence at the start, add a fade-out at the end, loop the chorus for a longer cut, or layer a sound effect over a song — all in your browser, all without leaving the app. Save the result to your cloud library when it's ready.

### AI Analog Warmth

**What it does.** AI Analog Warmth fixes the cold, brittle, "digital" sound that AI-generated tracks sometimes have. It listens to your audio, figures out exactly what's wrong (too harsh, too thin, too flat), and runs it through a chain of analog-style processing — gentle saturation, body EQ, smooth compression, and a touch of high-frequency softening — to make it sound like it was mixed on real analog gear. There's a dedicated **vocal mode** that protects breath and presence when you're warming up a vocal track, and an **intensity slider** so you control how much of the effect is applied.

**How it benefits you.** Your tracks stop sounding "obviously AI" and start sounding mixed. Listeners hear warmth, depth, and a finished feel. Marketing teams use it to make demos sound like masters; producers use it to add the analog character they'd otherwise need expensive outboard gear for.

### AI Style Enhancer

**What it does.** AI Style Enhancer applies a genre-matched mastering chain to your audio so it takes on the sonic identity of the style you choose. Pick from six presets — **Lo-Fi**, **EDM**, **Cinematic**, **Pop**, **Chill**, **Vintage** — and an intensity slider lets you blend the effect from subtle polish all the way to full transformation. EDM, Cinematic, and Pop also widen the stereo image; Vintage adds analog saturation; Lo-Fi adds the bit-crush, chorus, and tape feel everyone associates with the genre.

**How it benefits you.** One click takes a generic track and gives it a clear genre identity. You can audition the same song as a lo-fi study beat, a cinematic trailer cue, and a club-ready EDM banger — and pick whichever fits the project. Great for creators who want to repurpose one piece of music for many contexts.

### AIME — Automated AI Music Editor (Auto Trim)

**What it does.** AIME finds the best section of any track and trims it to the exact length you need. You tell it *"give me a punchy 30-second drop for a TikTok"* or *"a 60-second chorus loop for a podcast intro"*, and it detects the tempo, maps out the structure (intro, verse, build, chorus, drop, bridge, outro), scores every candidate section, picks the best one with an LLM, and trims it with beat-synced crossfades so the cut never sounds awkward. If the section it picks is shorter than your target, AIME intelligently loops and rearranges segments to fill the time naturally. You can also override the AI's pick and choose a different candidate section yourself.

**How it benefits you.** No more scrubbing through a 4-minute song to find the 15 seconds you actually need. AIME does the listening, the analysing, the maths, and the editing for you, then lets you A/B compare its choice against the original. Editors, social-media creators, DJs, and podcasters get clean, beat-locked clips in seconds.

### Stem Separation

**What it does.** Stem Separation takes a finished song and pulls it apart into its individual instruments: **vocals**, **drums**, **bass**, and **other** (everything else — guitars, keys, synths). You upload a track, the system processes it, and you get four clean audio files you can mix, remix, or use independently.

**How it benefits you.** Want an instrumental version of your favourite song? Pull the vocals. Want to remix only the drums? Extract them. Need a clean a-cappella for a mashup? It's there. Stem Separation unlocks every layer of a track so you can use them however you want — for remixes, karaoke, sampling, or learning how a song was put together.

## Production

Tools that take a finished track and prepare it for the real world — streaming services, podcasts, films, and more.

### Platform Mastering

**What it does.** Platform Mastering tunes your track to the loudness, dynamics, and tonal targets that each major platform expects — Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, TikTok, broadcast, cinema, and more. Pick a platform profile, run it on your audio, and you get back a master that meets that platform's specifications, ready to upload.

**How it benefits you.** No more guessing. No more "why is my track quieter than every other song on Spotify?" or "why did YouTube turn down my video?" Platform Mastering does the engineering work for you so your music plays back at the right level, with the right punch, on every service your audience uses.

### Reference Track Matching

**What it does.** Reference Track Matching takes a song you love — your reference — and makes your track sound more like it. The system analyses the vibe, energy, and sonic character of the reference, generates a tailored prompt that captures what makes it special, and uses that to guide a new generation that lands closer to the feel you're chasing.

**How it benefits you.** "I want it to sound like *that song*" is finally a workable instruction. Instead of struggling to describe a vibe in words, you point at an example, and the system handles the translation. Producers, supervisors, and content creators use it to nail a specific aesthetic without weeks of trial and error.

### Podcast Producer

**What it does.** Podcast Producer is a one-stop pipeline that turns spoken-word audio into a polished podcast episode — clean voice, balanced loudness, music beds where appropriate, and a final master ready for distribution. Run it once, save it to your library, ship it.

**How it benefits you.** Solo creators and small teams get a clean, professional-sounding episode without owning audio engineering skills or paying a producer. What used to take hours of post-production now takes minutes.

## Account & Workspace

Everything around your account, your saved work, your billing, and your job status.

### Sign-in & Accounts

**What it does.** Sign in with a secure account that keeps your work, your library, and your billing tied to you. Account creation, sign-in, and profile management are handled through a trusted authentication provider, and your information is synced safely across the app.

**How it benefits you.** One account unlocks everything: your generations are saved automatically, you can pick up where you left off on any device, and your billing and tokens follow you. No copy-pasting download links into a folder somewhere — your work is always *there* when you come back.

### Projects

**What it does.** Projects are folders for your work. Group related tracks, edits, lyrics, sound effects, and albums under a project — by client, by song, by season, by film — and everything you create inside a project stays organised together.

**How it benefits you.** Instead of a flat list of every audio file you've ever made, you get clean separation between work streams. Find that podcast episode from last month in two clicks; keep your client work separate from your personal experiments; archive a finished project without losing track of its pieces.

### User Library

**What it does.** Your User Library is the single place where everything you've created lives — every generated song, every lyric, every sound effect, every edit, every album, every stem separation, every podcast. Browse, search, filter, preview, and download any piece of content you've made.

**How it benefits you.** Nothing gets lost. Whether you generated a track five minutes ago or five months ago, it's still in your library, still streamable, still downloadable. The library is also where you re-use old work — pull a lyric you wrote last year into a new song, remix a track from your archives, or extend a clip you'd forgotten about.

### Tokens & Billing

**What it does.** AI Music Gen runs on a token system. Each feature costs a set number of tokens to use, and your account holds a balance you can top up or replenish through a subscription. You can see your current balance, your transaction history (which feature used how many tokens, and when), and your subscription plan from your account.

**How it benefits you.** Predictable, transparent pricing. You always know what something will cost before you run it, you can plan your usage around your balance, and you can pick the plan that matches how much you create — pay-as-you-go for occasional users, subscription for power users.

### Job Queue & Status

**What it does.** Generation tasks (music, albums, sound effects, stem separation) run in the background. The Job Queue gives you visibility into every task: what's queued, what's in progress, what's completed, and what failed. Each track in your library shows a live status as it progresses from queued, to in-progress, to ready.

**How it benefits you.** You're never left wondering "is it done yet?" The status updates automatically, you can leave the app and come back later, and if something fails you find out clearly instead of staring at a spinner forever. For long jobs like full album generation, the queue is how you keep an eye on every track at once.
